Why it exists

The internet won't always be there.

Most AI tools assume a working connection. Grid Down doesn't. Whether you're off-grid by choice or circumstance — power outages, hurricanes, blizzards, infrastructure failure — you still need access to knowledge.

And beyond outages, control metters. Grid Down runs entirely on your machine, so you're not dependent on external systems - no network required, no credits, and nothing leaving your environment.

How it's different

Not just documents. Answers.

Most portable offline solutions give you a pile of PDFs. Grid Down gives you a knowledge base that you can add to which is paired with an AI that has read all of them — and can answer your specific question with citations, step-by-step protocols, and links source with page and chapter identifiers (when available).

Ask it how to treat a wound when there's no hospital. Ask about water purification. Ask how to read a topographic map. It knows — and it shows its work.
