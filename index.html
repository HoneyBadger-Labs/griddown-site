Example Query

How do I can tomatoes?

Overview
Canning tomatoes allows for long-term storage while maintaining safety and quality.

Process
1. Prepare tomatoes (wash, blanch, peel)
2. Sterilize jars and lids
3. Add acid (lemon juice or citric acid)
4. Process in boiling water bath

Key Considerations
Proper acidity is required for safe storage. Processing time varies by altitude.

Source
USDA Complete Guide to Home Canning (stored locally)
